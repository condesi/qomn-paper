# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

As contributors and maintainers of the QOMN project, we pledge to make participation in this repository a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of experience level, gender identity, orientation, disability, personal appearance, body size, race, ethnicity, age, religion, or nationality.

## Expected behavior

- Use welcoming and inclusive language.
- Be respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Gracefully accept constructive criticism.
- Focus on what is best for the project and the broader community of engineers, researchers, and students who may use this work.
- Show empathy towards other community members.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment of any kind, public or private.
- Personal attacks on contributors or authors.
- Publishing others' private information without permission.
- Off-topic political or ideological discussions in issues and pull requests. This repository is strictly a working artifact for an academic and engineering preprint, and discussions must stay on that topic.
- Trolling, insulting comments, or deliberate disruption.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying standards of acceptable behavior and will take appropriate corrective action in response to any violation.

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the maintainer at `percy.rojas@condesi.pe`. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated. The project maintainer is obligated to maintain the confidentiality of the reporter.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies both within project spaces (issues, pull requests, commit messages) and in public spaces when an individual is representing the project.
